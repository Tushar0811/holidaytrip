Earn and Travel

We live in a wonderful world that is full of beauty, charm and adventure. There is no end to the adventures we can have if only we seek them with our eyes open.

To travel is to learn

For me travelling is an essential thing which not only makes you feel alive but rather it also connects to the world as well as the people around the world.

If you will never go, you will never know

I personally believe no matter how much you watch or listen about a particular place you can never get certain amount of satisfaction unless you yourself visit it once.

North-East India

North-Eastern side of India is so beautiful, yet it remains unexplored even among Indian travellers. Probably we don’t even know some of the areas that exist in the region. So far even if you may have heard of North East India, it could have been mainly for all the wrong reasons, which have affected the North East tourism in some way or the other. But there is so much to explore there that one fine day when you will find out about it, it will be nothing less than an amazement.

NorthEast India has suffered from years long political and ethinic movements. There has also been tribal identity conflicts in the region, however, since the past decade it has become not just been safe but an offbeat and great place to travel to.

Andaman And Nicobar

This place is such a beauty which if i try to mention in my words would do injustice to that place. I adore India is itself a beauty beyond imagination and this place do make sure it contributes 100% to it. Last year, back in 2019 I went to Andaman and Nicobar to relax and to earn a vibe to push myself forward in the run of life. This place has perfectly binded the diversity in this world of modernisation. You could even find Adivasis there and this amazed me a lot. The natural beauty of this place could not be explained. The sea shores are so natural that solely connects you to the nature.

Why not India?

I would not say foreign places do not offer tourism or there aren't better places to visit. But it would be a deeper injustice if you do not travel India. I am a proud human that I have been born in India. India is such a happy place, there's a lot of diversity and you could find different people and language and culture and positive vibes everywhere you go.

I can travel around in this country any number of times. No matter whether you visit a place one time or n times, you would surely enjoy the vibe and it has always been a new experinece for me. This land has always offered me something new to learn always. This country didn't only give me a bunch of good vibes and zeal to travel but also a strong reason to travel and learn yourself more.
